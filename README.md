# Firewood Rack

3D design for a welded steel firewood rack.

**[View Interactive 3D Model](index.html)**

## Specs

- **30"W x 16"D x 60"H** (5 feet tall)
- **4 sections** with diagonal shelf bars
- **1.5" square tube** construction
- **6" floor clearance**
- **Wall mount brackets** on back
- **Front-to-back bottom braces** for rigidity

## Cut List

| Qty | Length | Part |

| Qty | Size | Part |

## Materials

**Total 1.5" square tube needed:** ~57 feet (get 60' for margin)

**Finish:** Matte black powder coat

## Diagonal Bar Notes

- Cut ends at ~23° angle for clean fit against posts
- Sections 1 & 3: angle goes ↗ (bottom-left to top-right)
- Sections 2 & 4: angle goes ↖ (bottom-right to top-left)

## Assembly

1. Weld bottom frame (rectangle)
2. Attach 4 legs pointing down
3. Weld 4 corner posts pointing up
4. Attach front-to-back bottom braces between legs
5. Weld top frame
6. Weld diagonal bars (alternating direction per section)
7. Weld wall mount brackets to back
8. Powder coat
